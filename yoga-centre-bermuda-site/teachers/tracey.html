About Tracey

Teaching philosophy & approach

Hello, my name is Tracey! I am a 200-hour Yoga Teacher, having obtained my training through Mindful Integration in Bermuda in 2014. In 2019, I became a 30-hour Yoga Alliance certified Accessible Yoga Teacher — training that equipped me to teach yoga to students of all ages, sizes, abilities, and experiences.

I am also a Wellness Coach. In 2022, I completed an Ayurveda Wellness Coach certification through the Shakti School of Ayurveda. As a wellness coach, I employ the principles of Ayurveda to facilitate deep self-healing with heart and compassion — a heart-centred approach rooted in women's wisdom traditions and holistic medicine.

My interest, influence, and training have steered me toward working with yoga students one-on-one, incorporating Eastern and Western healing modalities with yoga practices. I am currently in the process of becoming a Foundational Yoga Therapy practitioner through breathingdeeply.com, with the intention of becoming a C-IAYT certified Yoga Therapist accredited by the International Association of Yoga Therapy in due course.

You can find me teaching at The Yoga Centre on the 1st and 3rd Sunday of the month, 9–10am. My class is Vinyasa Flow with a Mindfulness Touch.
