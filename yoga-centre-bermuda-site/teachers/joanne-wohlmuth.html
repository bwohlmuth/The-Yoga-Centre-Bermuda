About

Teaching philosophy & background

Joanne Wohlmuth is a co-founder of The Yoga Centre Bermuda and a devoted student of Sri Swami Satchidananda. She has been teaching yoga in Bermuda for over four decades, offering classes in Hatha yoga, Yoga Therapy, and meditation. Her teaching is grounded in the Integral Yoga tradition — compassionate, accessible, and rooted in the full spectrum of yogic practice.

Specialties

  • Yoga Therapy
  • Meditation
