About Calvina

Teaching philosophy & approach

Hello, my name is Calvina! I am a 200-hour Yoga Alliance certified Yoga Teacher (RYT 200), having completed my training in 2018 at The Yoga Centre through the Yoga on the Rock Teacher Training programme. I am also a Reiki-trained Energy Healer — and I love energy work!

You will find me where both positive and negative energies converge and balance. My approach weaves together the physical and energetic dimensions of practice, creating space for deep rest, restoration, and healing.

Because of my busy work schedule, I currently offer one-on-one sessions. In the Fall of 2026, I look forward to beginning a 45-minute yoga session every other Friday — on the first and third Friday of the month — to help usher you into your weekend. Time TBD.
