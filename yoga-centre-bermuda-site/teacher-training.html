About the programme

A transformative journey to the Self

  • Our programme is likely the most comprehensive teacher training being offered in Bermuda.
  • Your lead teacher holds senior yoga teaching credentials recognised both locally and internationally — MA, E-RYT 500, YACEP, C-IAYT.
  • Yoga on the Rock is a Registered Yoga School (RYS) under Yoga Alliance. Students who complete the programme receive an immediate Yoga Alliance certificate upon graduation.
  • Students learn and practise traditional Hatha yoga, Flow yoga, Chair yoga, Children's yoga, Chakra yoga, and Adaptive/Restorative yoga with a mindfulness approach — a unique combination drawn from the head teacher's experience and training.
  • Beyond the physical poses, a major programme focus is yoga philosophy and spirituality, wellness, and diversity and social justice — key components encouraged in Yoga Alliance teacher training programmes.
  • The programme is a deep dive into the many aspects of yoga under the Sivananda lineage tradition. For most students, it is a life-changing experience like no other.
  • The head teacher has over 45 years of experience practising, teaching, and studying yoga.

Please note: An interview is required before acceptance into the programme is confirmed. This ensures the training is the right fit for you and your intentions.

To find out if this training is right for you, reach out to Joanne directly.
